null Kolkata

Open Source Security Community
West Bengal,IN.

null is India's largest open security community. Registered as a non-profit society in 2010, we have been active since even before that. null is about spreading information security awareness.

Things We Do

This being a community,it's driven by volunteers and a bunch of super passionate InfoSec guys. We aim at driving the values of InfoSec in college students,individuals and corporates by arranging regular meetups. In this ever evolving world, where data is the most precious asset,we seek to grow more awareness by the following means

  • n|l Bachaav
  • n|l Job Portal
  • n|l Puliya
  • n|l Monthly Meets
  • n|l Humla
  • n|l Community Particpation
